Our Vision

Glory to Glory was birthed out of a desire to see more people experience the mainfest presence of God is their lives. We believe and hold to the scripture: Revelation 19:10 "For the testimony of Jesus is the spirit of prophecy." This scripture lays out the truth that the same power and grace that was present at the time of a persons healing, deliverance, salvation, God encounter, etc. , is readily available to anyone who hears it when they share of what Jesus did in their life.


This website is designed to encourage, comfort and strengthen you as a believer in Jesus Christ. If you need a touch from God, you have come to the right place. The ancient meaning of the word testimony in its root means ‘do it again/ repeat’ we believe that the power of testimony lies in the fact it depicts the nature of our God. When we give a testimony of a healing for instance, we depict the fact that He is our Healer. So please as you watch these videos and/or read these testimonies expect God to show up in your life and to do the same thing He did for someone else.

Testimonies

We have collected hundreds of testimonies over the years and have organized them by the type of healing, breakthrough or experience. You can either search a testimony by keyword by using the search bar in the top left side of the webpage or you can click the button below and scroll through the testimonies yourself. We hope that one or more testimonies will meet you at your place of need and that God will move in your life to bring about a similar end result. Whatever it is you are after, please know that we agree in faith with you and want to see God's will manifest in your life!

*Each and every written testimony has been submitted to us and have not been altered in any way except to format how they are presented in writing. All video testimonies have been either sumbitted by the public or captured by our video team. None of the video testimonies have been altered in any way except to present them in a more concise and clear way.
